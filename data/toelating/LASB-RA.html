You can apply to UCR if you have any of the following:

  • VWO diploma

English proficiency

You also need to prove your proficiency in English. You can do so by submitting an English test:

  • IELTS: 7.0 overall with none of the individual scores below 6.5
  • TOEFL: 94 (Internet based)
  • CAE (C1 Advanced): B
  • CPE (C2 Proficiency): C

Did you complete your high school in English? Then you are exempted from this requirement. Are you applying with a VWO or Abitur? Then you need to get a minimum score for English to be exempted. More info can be found on our website: www.ucr.nl .
