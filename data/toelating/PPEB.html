What degree do you have?

Admission is possible on the basis of either a VWO-diploma or, if you cannot fulfil this requirement, on the basis of a successfully passed first year of HBO with supplementary proof.

VWO-diploma

A VWO diploma including Mathematics A or B ( wiskunde A or wiskunde B ) and a grade of 8 or higher for English. If you have not obtained your diploma at the time of applying, the grade on your final 5VWO-report will be used. If your English grade on your most recent VWO transcript, at the time of the submission, is likely to be below 8.0, you can prove your English proficiency via other factual evidence. Proof of mathematical knowledge:

You demonstrate a pre-university secondary school level knowledge of Mathematics A or B (wiskunde A of B). If you cannot, one way to do this is to take an examination;

  • Boswell-Bèta (in Dutch) ,
  • CCVX exam in Mathematics A or B ,
  • OMPT-A Mathematics exam with a minimum score of 75% (7,5).  Note that you have a maximum of three attempts to pass this exam.

Please note the OMPT-A exam can only be used for admission to the Bachelor’s programmes Philosophy, Politics and Economics & Economics and Business Economics at Utrecht University. It will not be accepted to gain admission to other programmes.)

If you wish to use any of these options, you will have to pay for the test yourself.

University of Applied Sciences (HBO)

Did you complete the first year of a Dutch university of applied sciences (HBO) degree programme or do you have an HBO degree certificate? Then you are eligible for admission if you meet the requirements for Mathematics and English. You can do this by completing any of the tests described above with the minimum score, or by completing a state examination ( staatsexamen, through the medium of Dutch) administered by the Dutch Education Executive Agency (DUO: Dienst Uitvoering Onderwijs). For English, you must obtain an English grade of 8.0 minimum. For Wiskunde A or B, the minimum grade is a 5.5 (a pass).

English language requirements

Utrecht University uses the EMI (English Medium of Instruction) system to assess applicants’ English language skills, which distinguishes three proficiency levels: EMI-ready , EMI-experienced and EMI-advanced .

The required English level for admission to this Bachelor’s programme is EMI-advanced.

Visit our EMI-advanced page for Bachelor's degree programmes to find the accepted tests and required scores. Here, you can also see which diplomas qualify for an exemption from this requirement.
